The Facts

Also called the lesser panda, the red bear-cat, and the red cat-bear, is a mammal native to the eastern Himalayas and southwestern China.



Quick Facts
  • Common Name: Red Panda
  • Scientific Name: Ailurus fulgens
  • Type: Mammals
  • Diet: Herbivores
  • Average life span in the wild: 8 years
